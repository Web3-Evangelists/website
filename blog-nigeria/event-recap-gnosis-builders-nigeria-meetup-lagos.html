Event Recap - Gnosis Builders Nigeria Meetup Lagos

September 25, 2023

Written by: Uchenna Agams
‍

The Gnosis Builders Nigeria workshop held in Lagos brought together experts, enthusiasts, and industry leaders to discuss the evolving landscape of blockchain technology. With a diverse panel of speakers, the event covered topics ranging from Gnosis Chain validation to the challenges and opportunities in Nigeria's blockchain space.


The event was packed with informative sessions anchored by the Community Lead of Gnosis Builders Labaran Jamilu each led by accomplished professionals in the blockchain field. Here’s a glimpse;

‍

Labaran welcomed the attendees and set the tone for the day. He kicked off the event with an introductory and networking session that allowed attendees the chance to connect and exchange ideas. In this segment, some attendees shared their personal journeys in the Web3 space. These narratives provided inspiration and showed the diverse paths one can take in this very dynamic industry.

The next item on the menu was a comprehensive introduction to the Gnosis chain and its ecosystem anchored by Uchenna Onuegbu. In this session, insights were shared on the importance of the Gnosis chain and its significant contributions to the blockchain ecosystem.

‍

Olu took the stage to clearly explain the process of becoming a Gnosis Chain validator. His insights provided a valuable roadmap for those looking to participate in the network's validation process. Most attendees were pleasantly surprised to hear about the numerous benefits attached to becoming a Gnosis Chain validator, hence indicating interest in running a node. Olu’s interactive session allowed participants to seek clarification and gain deeper insights into the role of validators in the Gnosis Chain.

‍

The panel, moderated by Labaran, featured distinguished speakers including Idris, Nimi, Gilbert, and Olu. They delved into key topics surrounding blockchain development, crypto analysis, and community building. Insights were drawn from these very cerebral discussions. Among the highlights were;

For Blockchain Developers,

  • The panelists emphasized the importance of continuous learning and staying updated in the rapidly evolving blockchain field.
  • Decentralization was recognized as a pivotal aspect of their projects, offering benefits such as increased security and transparency, albeit with its set of challenges.
  • The fear associated with crypto and blockchain among Nigerians was attributed to a lack of awareness and misinformation.

‍



For Analysts:

  • The panelists highlighted unique factors influencing cryptocurrency adoption in Nigeria, including economic instability and a desire for financial inclusion.
  • They advised potential investors to conduct thorough research and diversify their portfolios to manage risk effectively.

‍

For Communities, Community Builders, and Users:

  • Focus on fostering community and growth within the Crypto/Web3 sector, providing valuable resources and support for users in the industry.
  • Collaboration within the community, education, and continuous innovation were highlighted as critical factors for success in the era of Web3

‍

The event shed light on the burgeoning opportunities and challenges in Nigeria's blockchain space. The event provided a platform for meaningful discussions, networking, and insights into the blockchain landscape in Nigeria. With a diverse range of speakers, attendees left with a deeper understanding of the potential and the path forward in this dynamic industry.
