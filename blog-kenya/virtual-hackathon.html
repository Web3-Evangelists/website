Unleashing Innovation: Join the Gnosis Builders Kenya Virtual Hackathon

August 11, 2023

Are you passionate about blockchain technology, eager to showcase your coding prowess, and looking to be part of a community of like-minded developers? Look no further! Gnosis Builders Kenya is thrilled to announce its upcoming virtual hackathon, set to ignite the world of blockchain innovation. Get ready to dive into the fascinating world of Gnosis Chain and create groundbreaking projects that have the potential to revolutionize the blockchain landscape.

Event Details

  • Hackathon Kickoff: August 12th
  • Project Submission Deadline: September 9th, 11:59pm
  • Finale Ceremony: September 10th

Unveiling Gnosis Chain: A Platform for Creativity and Innovation

Gnosis Builders Kenya, in collaboration with the Gnosis Builders development team, is excited to invite you to a thrilling virtual hackathon experience. Gnosis Chain, a cutting-edge blockchain platform, serves as the backdrop for this event. As you delve into this hackathon, you'll have the unique opportunity to explore the endless possibilities that Gnosis Chain has to offer and push the boundaries of what can be achieved in the world of blockchain technology.

Prizes and Recognition

Your innovation and hard work won't go unnoticed! Gnosis Builders Kenya is offering a total of $1000 in prizes for the most innovative projects built on Gnosis Chain. This is your chance to not only showcase your skills but also grab some well-deserved rewards for your efforts. Whether you're an experienced developer or a newcomer to the world of blockchain, this hackathon is the perfect platform to shine.

Prize Breakdown:

$1000 USD total prizes for the most innovative projects built on Gnosis Chain:

  • 1st Prize - $500
  • 2nd Prize - $300
  • 3rd Prize - $200

Empowering Workshops

Throughout the hackathon, the Gnosis Builders development team will be your guiding lights. They will be hosting a series of workshops designed to empower you with the knowledge and skills needed to build on Gnosis Chain. From understanding the intricacies of the platform to exploring the various tools within the Gnosis Chain ecosystem, these workshops are a golden opportunity to learn from the experts and enhance your capabilities.

Agenda:

  • Gnosis Chain Technical Deep Dive Workshop
  • Sunday, August 20th @ 12:00 PM
  • Gnosis Chain NFT Ecosystem Workshop
  • Wednesday, August 23th @ 7:30 PM
  • Gnosis Chain DeFi Workshop
  • Sunday, August 27th @ 12:00 PM
  • Gnosis Chain DAO Tooling Workshop
  • Wednesday, August 30th @ 7:30 PM
  • Project Submissions Due
  • Saturday, September 9th @ 11:59 PM
  • Closing Ceremony and Prize Giving
  • Sunday, September 10th @ 12:00 PM

Crafting Your Innovation: Steps to Participate

  1. Registration: To embark on this exciting journey, first, register for the hackathon. Don't miss the kickoff on August 12th!
  2. Sign up here
  3. Ideation and Creation: The hackathon window provides ample time for you to brainstorm, ideate, and bring your innovative project to life on Gnosis Chain.
  4. Guidance and Learning: Participate in the enlightening workshops hosted by the Gnosis Builders development team. Gain insights and explore the tools that will empower your project creation.
  5. Building and Refining: Roll up your sleeves and start coding! Bring your vision to life, refine your project, and create something that will captivate the judges and the blockchain community.
  6. Submission: September 9th is the deadline for project submissions. Make sure to submit your masterpiece by 11:59pm to be considered for the prizes.
  7. The Grand Finale: On September 10th, join us for the finale ceremony where the most innovative projects will be celebrated and winners will be announced.

Fueling Innovation Together: Join Us!

The Gnosis Builders Kenya virtual hackathon is your chance to not only showcase your skills but to collaborate with a community that shares your passion for blockchain and decentralized technologies. It's a golden opportunity to learn, create, and stand a chance to win remarkable prizes.

So mark your calendars, clear your schedule, and get ready to embark on a journey that will fuel your creativity, challenge your abilities, and connect you with fellow developers who are as enthusiastic as you are We can't wait to see the incredible projects you'll create on Gnosis Chain. Let the hacking begin!
