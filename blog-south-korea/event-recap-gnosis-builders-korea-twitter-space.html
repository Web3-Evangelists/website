Event Recap - Gnosis Builders Korea Twitter Space

September 11, 2023

On August 31st, Gnosis Builders South Korea hosted an engaging and informative Twitter Space event featuring guest speakers from Coineasy, SojuDAO, and Gnosis Chain. This Twitter Space was the first of it's kind for Gnosis Builders South Korea, and proved to be a resounding success, providing valuable insights into Gnosis Chain and its unique advantages.

Agenda:

  1. Speaker's Introduction: The event began with an introduction of the guest speakers, including Elizabeth and Sharon, representing Gnosis Builders, the representative from Coineasy, and Prince John representing SojuDAO. Each speaker's role within the blockchain industry was highlighted.
  2. Understanding Gnosis Chain: Attendees gained a comprehensive understanding of Gnosis Chain, its architecture, and its role within the broader blockchain ecosystem.
  3. Advantages of Gnosis over Ethereum: The speakers delved into the distinct advantages that Gnosis offers over the Ethereum network, emphasizing factors such as scalability and cost-efficiency.
  4. Choosing Gnosis over Layer 2: An essential question was explored - why developers should consider deploying smart contracts on Gnosis Chain instead of opting for Layer 2 scaling solutions.
  5. Gnosis vs. Layer 2 Solutions: The competitive positioning of Gnosis in relation to Layer 2 solutions like Aptos and Sui was discussed, highlighting Gnosis's unique features and benefits.
  6. Consensus Mechanism: Technical enthusiasts learned about the consensus mechanism underpinning Gnosis Chain, providing insights into its security and efficiency.
  7. Role of Gnosis Builders: The event detailed the pivotal role played by Gnosis Builders within the blockchain space, promoting innovation and development.
  8. Web 3 Evangelists: Information regarding the Web 3 Evangelists, including Elizabeth and Sharon, their responsibilities, and contributions to the ecosystem, was shared with the audience.
  9. Becoming a Web3 Evangelist: Aspiring Web3 Evangelists received valuable insights into the journey and requirements for becoming a part of this vital community.

The session featuring Prince John, representing SojuDAO, continued with an in-depth exploration of the project and its association with Gnosis Chain:

  1. Introduction: Prince John introduced himself and set the stage for discussions about SojuDAO, providing context to the audience.
  2. SojuDAO's Mission: Attendees learned about the mission and objectives of SojuDAO within the blockchain realm, highlighting its unique vision.
  3. SojuDAO's Project on Gnosis: Prince John shared details about SojuDAO's project hosted on Gnosis Chain, offering insights into the specific use cases and goals of the project.
  4. Choosing Gnosis: The session emphasized why SojuDAO opted to build its project on Gnosis Chain, showcasing the alignment between Gnosis's strengths and SojuDAO's vision.
  5. Strengths of Gnosis: Prince John elaborated on the strengths and advantages of Gnosis Chain compared to other blockchain networks, underscoring the value it brings to projects like SojuDAO.
  6. Tools and Resources: The event explored the extensive tools and resources provided by Gnosis Chain to support developers and projects like SojuDAO, fostering growth and innovation.
  7. Building Experience: Prince John shared valuable insights and experiences from SojuDAO's journey of building on Gnosis Chain, offering a practical perspective.
  8. Gnosis vs. Ethereum: Notable distinctions between Gnosis Chain and Ethereum, particularly in terms of smart contract deployment and performance, were discussed in detail.
  9. Guides and Advice: Prince John provided practical guides, tutorials, and valuable advice for individuals and developers interested in embarking on their own projects within the Gnosis ecosystem, sharing knowledge and best practices.

Additionally, the representative from Coineasy contributed to the event with insights into the Coineasy platform and its relationship with Gnosis Chain:

  1. Introduction: The Coineasy representative introduced themselves and provided context about Coineasy's role within the cryptocurrency and blockchain space.
  2. Coineasy's Integration with Gnosis: Attendees learned about how Coineasy integrates with Gnosis Chain, providing valuable services or solutions to the Gnosis ecosystem.
  3. Benefits of Coineasy's Integration: The session highlighted the benefits and advantages that users and developers can experience by using Coineasy in conjunction with Gnosis Chain.
  4. Coineasy's Perspective on Gnosis: The representative shared Coineasy's perspective on Gnosis Chain, showcasing the platform's commitment to innovation and collaboration within the blockchain industry.

In conclusion, the Gnosis Builders Twitter Space event on August 31st provided an extensive and insightful overview of Gnosis Chain and its importance within the blockchain landscape. The participation of Elizabeth and Sharon from Gnosis Builders, along with Prince John from SojuDAO and the representative from Coineasy, added rich layers of practical experience and demonstrated the collaborative potential within the Gnosis ecosystem. Despite technical challenges, the event successfully educated and inspired the Web 3.0 community, fostering discussions and collaborations in the ever-evolving blockchain space.
