Unleash Your Innovation at the APUBCC Sparkathon: Sponsored by Gnosis Builders Malaysia

September 25, 2023

Gnosis Builders Malaysia is very excited to announce we are sponsoring the APUBCC Sparkathon, a week long, hybrid hackathon for students and developers alike. The APUBCC Sparkathon is the perfect opportunity for you to showcase your talent and creativity. Organized by the Asia Pacific University Blockchain and Cryptocurrency Club (APUBCC), this hackathon promises a thrilling adventure filled with hacking, networking, learning, and, of course, a lot of fun!

The Sparkathon Journey

The APUBCC Sparkathon is not your ordinary hackathon. It begins online, on September 22, 2023, and continues until September 30, 2023, at 11:59 p.m. MYT. During this period, participants will have the chance to work on their projects remotely, collaborate with like-minded individuals, and dive deep into the realms of blockchain, AI, and decentralized finance.

But that's not all! On October 1, 2023, participants will have the unique opportunity to gather physically at Asia Pacific University for the demo day. It's a chance to put faces to the names you've been collaborating with online, showcase your innovative solutions, and learn from others in the field.

To help participants get started, the hackathon organizers are hosting a series of workshops from September 22 to September 24, 2023, and on September 30, 2023. These workshops will provide invaluable insights into the hackathon's problem statements and offer guidance on how to leverage blockchain technology to create groundbreaking solutions.

The Sparkathon Challenges

This hackathon presents a set of exciting problem statements that participants can choose to work on:

1. AI: Bridging the Gap with Blockchain

Are you intrigued by the potential of decentralized AI in the world of web3? The first challenge invites you to harness the power of blockchain to innovate AI solutions. Explore how decentralized AI can redefine, showcase, and elevate its role within the crypto sphere. Show the world what's possible when AI and blockchain merge.

2. DeFi and Payments: Pioneering Decentralized Finance

The second problem statement beckons you to leave traditional finance behind and design new decentralized applications (dApps) that contribute to a more open and inclusive financial system. Your mission is to connect users to crypto by building projects that facilitate everyday payments in the crypto world.

3. Mobile Consumer: Bringing Crypto to Your Fingertips

In the age of mobile technology, it's time to make crypto truly accessible on the go. Challenge yourself to create mobile-first web3 projects that empower users to seamlessly interact with cryptocurrencies using their smartphones.

Prizes Worth Chasing

As if the thrill of competition and the chance to innovate weren't motivation enough, the APUBCC Sparkathon boasts an impressive total prize pool of over $10,000. And here's the exciting part: Gnosis Builders Malaysia is contributing $1,000 to reward the top three most innovative projects deployed on the Gnosis Chain.

Unlocking the Future of DeFi with Gnosis Builders Malaysia

Gnosis Builders Malaysia is not just offering prizes; they're also invested in your growth and success. On September 23, they hosted an in-person workshop, giving hackers a unique opportunity to delve into the future of decentralized finance (DeFi) on Gnosis Chain. This workshop offered valuable insights, guidance, and hands-on experience that will undoubtedly prove invaluable as you tackle the hackathon's challenges.

Join the Sparkathon Adventure

The APUBCC Sparkathon is your chance to make a mark in the world of blockchain, AI, and decentralized finance. Whether you're a coding prodigy or a creative thinker, there's a place for you in this hackathon.

So, don't miss this golden opportunity to learn, collaborate, and potentially change the future of the crypto landscape. Join the APUBCC Sparkathon and let your innovation shine!

Remember, the future of blockchain and decentralized technology is in your hands, and Gnosis Builders Malaysia is here to support you every step of the way. Join our Telegram support channel for any questions or blockers you may run into! Happy hacking!
