With an action-packed agenda, attendees are in for a treat as they unravel the mysteries of this groundbreaking technology.

Event Agenda:

6:00 PM - 6:15 PM: Welcome & Introduction

The evening kicks off with a warm welcome and introduction. It's a chance for participants to connect, network, and get to know their fellow blockchain enthusiasts. The atmosphere will be buzzing with excitement as everyone gears up for an educational and engaging experience.

6:15 PM - 6:25 PM: Overview of Gnosis

Get ready to dive headfirst into the world of Gnosis. During this session, you will gain valuable insights into the fundamentals of Gnosis, its history, and the vision that drives its development. Whether you're a seasoned blockchain expert or just dipping your toes into the crypto waters, this overview will set the stage for what's to come.

The heart of the event lies in this deep dive into Gnosis Chain. Led by experts in the field, this one-hour session will explore the intricacies and innovations that make Gnosis Chain stand out in the blockchain landscape. From its unique consensus mechanism to its applications in DeFi, NFTs, and more, this segment promises to be both educational and eye-opening.

7:25 PM - 7:35 PM: Q&A Session

Got burning questions about Gnosis Chain? Here's your chance to get answers. Our panel of experts will be on hand to address your inquiries and provide clarity on anything related to Gnosis and its blockchain technology.

7:35 PM - 8:00 PM: Networking

As the event winds down, the networking session begins. This is where the real magic happens. Whether you're looking for business opportunities or simply want to expand your knowledge, the networking segment is the perfect way to conclude this unforgettable evening.
