Gnosis Builders Kazakhstan AMA with Blockchain Center

August 2, 2023

Are you eager to delve into the world of Gnosis Chain and discover how Gnosis Builders are shaping the future of the blockchain network? Mark your calendars for an exciting Ask Me Anything (AMA) session, where Gnosis Builders Kazakhstan and Blockchain Center will be hosting an enlightening conversation about Gnosis Chain and their efforts to enhance its decentralization.

Unraveling Gnosis Chain: Redefining Blockchain Decentralization

On August 1st, 2023, at 7:30 PM UTC+6, we invite you to join us for a thought-provoking AMA session that promises to be both informative and engaging. Gnosis Builders Kazakhstan, a passionate team of blockchain enthusiasts, in collaboration with the esteemed Blockchain Center, is dedicated to revolutionizing the way we perceive blockchain networks and decentralization.

Gnosis Builders' Contribution to Decentralization

Gnosis Builders has been at the forefront of developing solutions that bolster the decentralization of the Gnosis Chain network. From creating robust validator nodes to fostering community engagement, they have played a pivotal role in shaping the network's decentralized ecosystem. During the AMA, the team will provide valuable insights into their ongoing projects and initiatives, emphasizing the importance of community participation in ensuring a truly decentralized network.

Your Chance to Participate

This AMA is not just an opportunity to passively listen to experts; it's a chance for you to be an active part of the conversation. Gnosis Builders and the Blockchain Center eagerly await your questions and curiosity. Whether you are a blockchain enthusiast, developer, or curious observer, your participation will enrich the discussion.

How to Join

Participating in the AMA is simple! On August 1st, 2023, at 7:30 PM UTC+6, head to the Blockchain Center’s Decentrathon Telegram Channel.
