Evolving From Gnosis Builders to Web3 Evangelists

October 10, 2023

We are thrilled to announce that we are expanding our horizons in our magnificent transformation from Gnosis Builders to Web3 Evangelists!

Before we dive into more details, we would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to our community that supported us during our time as Gnosis Builder Evangelists; Furthermore, we are excited about the opportunity to provide increased breadth and depth into the Web3 space with our transformation.

‍

So, why the Change?

‍

Our passion for blockchain, cryptocurrency, and decentralization has fueled our desire to take things to the next level. As Web3 Evangelists, our mission is clear: we want to spread the word about the immense potential of the decentralized web and inspire everyone to be a part of this thrilling journey.

‍

What You Can Expect from Web3 Evangelists:

‍

  • Engaging Content: Prepare yourself for a wealth of educational and entertaining content that will demystify the world of Web3. We're gearing up to deliver articles, videos, podcasts, and more to keep you informed and entertained.
  • Community Engagement: Our community has always been at the heart of what we do. We're committed to fostering a warm and welcoming space where Web3 enthusiasts can connect, share ideas, and collaborate on exciting projects.
  • Events & Workshops: Knowledge is power, and we want to empower you with the skills and insights you need to thrive in the Web3 era. Look forward to interactive events, webinars, and workshops.
  • Partnerships: We'll be partnering with like-minded organizations and experts to bring you the latest insights and opportunities in the Web3 space.
  • Fun and Surprises: Who doesn't love a bit of excitement? Brace yourself for contests, giveaways, and other surprises that will add a touch of thrill to your Web3 journey.

‍

This transformation isn't just a change in our name; it's a reflection of our evolving focus and unwavering commitment to the Web3 revolution. We want you to be an integral part of this exhilarating transition and to remain the heart and soul of our community.

‍
